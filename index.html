Who we are

Hyderco U.K. Limited provides a global expediting, procurement and logistics service helping clients to fulfil their requirements efficiently regardless of which part of the globe the goods are coming from.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to solve procurement and supply chain problems by offering one stop solutions to our customers and by providing excellent before and after sales services and with best delivery times.

OUR VISION

Our Vision is to become leading sourcing, supply and procurement consultancy in oil & gas, petrochemical, medical and security industries and help our clients to reduce bottom line costs.

OUR CORE VALUES

In pursuing our mission we will strive to act with Authenticity, Integrity, Openness, Courtesy, Responsiveness, Timeliness, Professionalism, and Consistency.

Since 1993, major players in Africa's Engineering, Construction, Fabrication, Installation, and Oil & Gas industry have put their trust in HYDERCO UK LTD for the provisioning of their supply chain and expediting needs.
